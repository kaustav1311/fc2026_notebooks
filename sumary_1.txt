# Project Goal
 
Build a **World Cup 2026 Fantasy Intelligence Engine** that:
 
* Creates a complete player universe for all 48 nations.
* Scores players daily using multiple models.
* Produces pools of recommended players rather than a single ranking.
* Uses betting odds, historical performance, form, matchup strength, weather, referee tendencies, and eventually FIFA fantasy ownership data.
* Supports daily optimization rather than season-long fantasy.
 
---
 
# User Inputs / Design Preferences
 
### 1. Single Player Universe
 
Not:
 
```text
player_gk
player_defender
player_midfielder
player_forward
```
 
Instead:
 
```text
wc26_players
```
 
One master player table.
 
Position-specific metrics live in metric columns.
 
---
 
### 2. Nation Mapping Is Critical
 
Need universal mapping because every source names countries differently.
 
Examples:
 
```text
South Korea
Korea Republic
Republic of Korea
KOR
```
 
```text
Türkiye
Turkey
TUR
```
 
```text
Côte d'Ivoire
Ivory Coast
CIV
```
 
```text
DR Congo
Democratic Republic of Congo
Congo DR
COD
```
 
Nation table must contain:
 
```text
all_names
all_aliases
all_abbreviations
source_specific_names
```
 
---
 
### 3. Referee Data Is High Priority
 
Need referee warehouse before tournament starts.
 
Desired metrics:
 


performance snapshots.
 
Potential sources:

* WorldReferee
